About

As a digital artist, I was frustrated that there was no way to prove
ownership and authenticity of my work. So, I set out to create
a solution. In 2014, I developed the concept of Non-Fungible Tokens
(NFTs). NFTs are special because they provide a unique way
to verify ownership of digital assets, enabling creators to monetize
their work. It’s exciting to see NFTs revolutionize the art world
and beyond, bringing more value to digital creations.
